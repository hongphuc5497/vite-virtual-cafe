# Experimentation Workflow

Experiments are allowed. Unbounded experiments are not.

## Start Template

```markdown
Name:
Hypothesis:
User pain:
Time box:
Kill date:
Continue evidence:
Files/repo:
Owner:
```

## Rules

- Max two active experiments.
- No new repo unless the experiment cannot fit here.
- No automation until the manual workflow works twice.
- No productization until a real user or repeated personal pain exists.

## Outcomes

- Promote: becomes product work.
- Park: useful later, not active.
- Kill: delete or archive, with one-line reason.
